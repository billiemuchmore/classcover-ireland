Yes. ClassCover Ireland uses the same platform you already know. Schools post bookings, substitute teachers respond through the app, and cover is confirmed in minutes. The core experience is identical — adapted for the Irish school system, including primary and secondary schools and SNAs (Special Needs Assistants).
ClassCover is launching in Ireland in 2026. Substitute teachers and SNAs who pre-register now will be among the first to get access and will be notified as we approach launch.
Yes. ClassCover is completely free for substitute teachers and SNAs in Ireland, just as it is in Australia. You create your profile, connect with local schools, and get booked at no cost. Schools pay a simple annual subscription — there are no placement fees and no per-booking charges.
Pre-register your interest using the button above. When ClassCover Ireland launches in 2026, we'll notify you directly so you can set up your Ireland profile and start connecting with local schools straight away. Being first on the list means you'll be visible to schools from day one.
ClassCover Ireland will be a fresh start on the Irish platform. You'll create a new Ireland profile when we launch, but your experience using ClassCover in Australia means you'll know exactly how the system works from day one — no learning curve.
In Ireland, substitute teachers are known as "substitute teachers" or "subs" — not relief teachers as in Australia. SNAs (Special Needs Assistants) are a distinct and important role in the Irish school system alongside substitute teachers. Irish schools follow Department of Education guidelines and the school year runs from September to June. ClassCover Ireland is built with these differences in mind so the platform works exactly as Irish schools and subs expect.
