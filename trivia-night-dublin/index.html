The night

Meet. Connect. Belong.

Teaching runs on connection. But subbing can mean a new school every week and a staffroom full of strangers. On the school side, there's rarely a spare minute to properly get to know the subs and SNAs who keep the day running.

This night fixes that. Subs, SNAs and school leaders in one room, drinks in hand, quiz on, prizes worth staying for. ClassCover is coming to Ireland, and this is the community's first night out. Be able to say you were there.

Who's invited

Know ClassCover already, or hearing about us for the first time? Either way, you're welcome. Come along on your own or bring colleagues. Just register everyone so we can save the seats.

Substitute teachers

Meet other subs, swap stories, and find your people beyond the staffroom.

SNAs

Special Needs Assistants, front and centre in this community from day one.

Principals & deputies

School leaders who want to connect with the subs and SNAs supporting their schools.
