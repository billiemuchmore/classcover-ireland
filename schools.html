How it works

Post an absence. Get a confirmed sub.

1

Post

Open the app, select the date, the role, and any notes for the substitute. Takes 30 seconds.

2

Match

ClassCover notifies available substitute teachers and SNAs in your area. On Lite, this goes to your own list. On the full platform, it reaches the wider network.

3

Confirmed

A qualified sub accepts the booking. In Australia, this takes an average of 2 minutes 17 seconds.

4

Sorted

The sub arrives with your lesson plans and induction documents already attached to the booking. No phone calls. No chasing.

Why county by county

ClassCover works best when schools and subs are in the same area.

A booking platform only works when there are enough substitute teachers nearby to fill the gaps. That is why we are rolling out county by county, building a strong teacher network in each area before expanding.

Your application helps us understand where the demand is strongest. The areas with the most school interest will be the first to launch.

This is the same approach that built ClassCover in Australia, where it started in one region of Sydney and now serves 1 in 2 schools nationally.
