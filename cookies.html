Cookie Policy

Version 1.0  ·  Effective date: 17 February 2026  ·  Classification: Public

About This Policy

ClassCover Pty Ltd ("ClassCover") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws. This Cookie Policy explains how ClassCover uses cookies and similar technologies on its platform and website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use an application. They help the site remember your preferences, keep your session secure, and understand how you interact with the platform. Similar technologies include pixel tags, web beacons, and local storage.

How We Use Cookies

ClassCover uses cookies to:

  • Support platform operations and maintain secure user sessions
  • Remember user preferences and settings
  • Analyse platform usage and improve performance
  • Improve the functionality of our services

These cookies may collect and process information such as IP address, browser type, device information, and system usage or interaction data.

Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Consent required?
Essential Required for the platform to function correctly. Enables login, secure sessions, privacy preference storage, form processing, and access to key platform services. Disabling these cookies may limit your ability to use the platform. No — these are strictly necessary
Performance & Analytics Used to analyse how the platform is used, diagnose technical problems, and improve performance. These cookies help us understand which features are most used and where users encounter issues. Yes — requires your consent
Functionality Used to personalise content, remember your preferences, and improve your experience on return visits. Yes — requires your consent
Third-Party Some cookies may be provided by authorised third-party service providers that support platform operations, analytics, performance monitoring, and security. Appropriate safeguards are implemented to ensure personal data is protected. Yes — requires your consent

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be provided by authorised third-party service providers that support ClassCover's platform operations, analytics, performance monitoring, and security. Where third parties are engaged, appropriate safeguards are implemented to ensure that personal data is protected and processed in accordance with applicable data protection requirements.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Cookies that are strictly necessary for the operation and security of the platform are used as required. All other cookies — including performance and functionality cookies — are used only based on your consent, which may be accepted, declined, or customised at any time.

You can manage or withdraw your cookie consent at any time by:

  • Using the cookie preference centre on this website (click "Manage cookies" in the footer)
  • Adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Using your device settings to manage permissions for mobile applications

Please note that disabling non-essential cookies will not affect the core operation of the platform, but may limit some personalisation and analytics features.

Your Rights

Individuals have the right to withdraw their cookie consent and to exercise their data protection rights, including access, correction, restriction, objection, or deletion of their personal data, subject to applicable legal requirements.

To exercise your data rights, please submit a request via our Data Subject Rights Request Form or contact us directly.
