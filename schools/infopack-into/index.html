  1. Promoter: Class Cover Pvt Limited (registered in the Republic of Ireland under the Companies Act 2014, company no. 819425), The Black Church, St. Mary's Place, Dublin 7, D07 P4AX, Ireland ("ClassCover").

  3. Eligibility: Open to staff members (including teachers, principals, deputy principals, and SNAs) of a recognised primary or post-primary school in the Republic of Ireland, aged 18+.
